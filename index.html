Questions

Is this safe / against Discord's rules?

It uses Discord's official Rich Presence API — the same one real games use. It's a harmless display trick, not a hack, and doesn't touch your account credentials.

Do I need the real GTA VI?

No. That's the whole joke — it shows the status without the game.

Will my friends know it's fake?

Not from your profile. It looks identical to actually playing.

Does it work on mobile / browser?

No — it needs the Discord desktop app open on Windows.
